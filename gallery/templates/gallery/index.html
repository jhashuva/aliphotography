About Me

Hello! Thank you for visiting Ali Photography! First of all, I love working people who do their own thing, and I want their photos to be an authentic expression of who they are!

I am so into making memories! The real ones, the once that pull at your heart strings, the ones that make you feel something! With my images, capturing those real moments that naturally unfold, that is what i really Love! It would be an absolute honour to capture your love, growth & those real moments that matter!

currently live in the Narasaraopet area, and I consider it a real opportunity to travel & meet different people throughout my job. Outside of the office my time is spent intentionally with family & friends....and most of the time i have to drag myself away from my computer! I love to read when i have the time.. which is rare. Anyways, check out my work, feel free to send me a message, & let's capture YOU!

with love
Ali Shaik
